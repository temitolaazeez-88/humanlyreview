FAQ

Clear answers to the common questions users ask before ordering, paying, submitting files, and receiving reports.

Is HumanlyReview reviewed by humans?

Yes. Technology helps collect submissions, organize reports and send notifications, but the final review is prepared by qualified human experts.

Do you rewrite my work?

No. HumanlyReview identifies issues, explains why they matter, and recommends what to improve. It is not a full rewrite button.

What exactly do I receive?

You receive two detailed PDF reports: an Error Identification Report and a Recommendation Report. The issue numbers match so advice is connected to each problem.

Why are there no free trials?

Human expert review takes time. Instead of a free trial, the site shows premium sample reports so users can see the quality before paying.

Why must I verify my email?

Your reports are private. Verification helps make sure the final PDFs go only to the correct email address.

Can I paste text into the website?

No. Writing submissions such as essays, CVs, reports and proposals must be uploaded as a document or shared through a secure link.

What can I submit?

Documents, websites, code, screenshots, images, videos, audio, AI-generated outputs, business materials, career materials, academic work and custom materials.

How are plans different?

Every plan includes detailed human expert review. Plans vary by page count, number of URLs, image count, media duration, code size, delivery time, follow-up and support.

What happens if I exceed a plan limit?

The system shows a clear message, explains what exceeded the limit and asks you to choose a higher plan, reduce the material or contact support after ordering.

Which payment gateway will I use?

The site automatically selects the best available payment option. Nigeria/NGN usually uses Paystack, while international payments usually use Flutterwave if configured by admin.

What if I paid but payment is not confirmed?

Go to Contact & Complaint, enter your Review ID, choose “Paid but not confirmed”, and submit the details. This keeps support tied to a real order and reduces spam.

Can I complain before paying?

General questions can use the contact email. Complaint tickets inside the website require a Review ID so admin can solve real payment, upload, delivery, report or account issues.

How long is my data stored?

Review data is stored for a limited time based on admin-controlled retention settings. Users can see notices and may request deletion, but retention rules are controlled by admin.

Can I delete my review or account?

Yes. Users can request deletion from the dashboard. Admin can also handle deletion according to the retention and security policy.

Can I submit AI-generated content?

Yes. Human experts can review AI-written documents, AI images, AI code, AI scripts, AI media and AI business content for quality issues and risks.

When will I receive my reports?

Delivery depends on your selected plan and admin settings. Your dashboard shows status, and reports are emailed to your verified email when ready.
